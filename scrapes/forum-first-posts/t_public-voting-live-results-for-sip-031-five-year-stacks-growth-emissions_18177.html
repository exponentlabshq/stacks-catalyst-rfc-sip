The Stacks community has until ~July 9 to vote on the proposal to create a treasury focused on accelerating ecosystem growth. Make sure to cast your vote and help shape the future of Stacks.

*SIP-031 Voting Window
Vote start: ~June 25th at estimated Bitcoin block 902,677
Vote end: ~July 9th at estimated Bitcoin block 904,113

Using Multiple Wallets or Accounts?
We understand many community members hold STX and do Stacking across multiple wallets or accounts. If you have STX stacked or unstacked in other wallets or addresses, you can repeat the process and vote from each one.

Liquid Staking Tokens
If you hold a liquid staking token (like stSTX, stSTXbtc, or LiSTX), your ability to vote depends on your staking provider.

Some providers may host their own votes to let users signal how they want their tokens to be used in governance. Others may not.

Check with your staking provider to see if they support voting. For example: StackingDAO will host a vote at app.stackingdao.com

If you also hold STX directly in your own wallet (e.g. Leather, Xverse), you can vote at stx.eco regardless of your staking provider.
